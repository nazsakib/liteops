Is SyncShell really free forever?

+

Yes! SyncShell is completely free. No hidden fees, no "premium plans", no credit card required. We use Google Sheets as the database, which is also free. The only cost would be if you exceed Google's generous free tier (15GB of storage), which most small businesses never hit.

Is my business data secure?

+

Absolutely. Your data is stored in YOUR Google Sheets account, protected by Google's bank-grade security (256-bit encryption). We never see, access, or store your business data on our servers. You have complete control and ownership.

Do I need internet connection to use SyncShell?

+

SyncShell works offline! You can create invoices, process sales, and manage inventory without internet. Everything syncs automatically when you reconnect to WiFi or mobile data.

Yes! Just share your Google Sheet with them like any other Google Doc. They can view it in real-time on their computer, make edits if you give them permission, or export reports. It's that simple.
